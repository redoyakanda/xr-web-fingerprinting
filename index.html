Academic browser measurement foundation

XR Web Fingerprinting Research Platform

This prototype collects browser-exposed navigator, screen, window, canvas, WebGL, audio, and WebGPU properties from XR/WebXR and traditional browsers for reproducible fingerprinting research. It intentionally avoids user input and motion sensor data in this research phase.

Ready to collect a browser fingerprint.

Excluded modules
